My name is Shutian Liu (柳舒恬 in Chinese). I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Economics &amp; Public Policy at [Imperial College Business School](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/business-school/), affiliated with the Centre for Health Economics and Policy Innovation. I am supervised by [Franco Sassi](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/people/f.sassi) at the Business School and [Verena Zuber](https://profiles.imperial.ac.uk/v.zuber) at the School of Public Health. I will be on the 2026&ndash;2027 academic job market.

My research explores how environmental and genetic factors jointly shape inequalities in human capital outcomes, particularly in health and education. Before Imperial, I received my M.Sc. in Applied Economics and Management from Cornell University in 2020, and my B.Sc. in Management Science and B.Econ. in Economics from Renmin University of China in 2017.
